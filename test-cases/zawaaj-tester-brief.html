1
Register as a new family
Register → verify → create profile → browse
Family user — new account
1
Go to zawaaj.uk and click Create your profile. You should see a choice: I'm a parent or guardian registering for a family member vs I'm registering for myself.
Expected: two clear options with distinct descriptions
2
Choose Parent or guardian and complete the contact details form. Use your real email address — you will receive a verification email.
Expected: multi-step form with progress indicator; required fields validated before proceeding
3
Submit the form. You should see a Check your inbox screen with your email address shown.
Expected: holding screen with resend option; no redirect to dashboard yet
4
Open your email and click the verification link. You should be redirected back to the platform.
Expected: brief success message, then redirect to profile creation prompt
5
Create a candidate profile — fill in all fields across the steps (personal details, faith, preferences, bio) and submit.
Expected: profile submitted confirmation; dashboard shows Your profile is being reviewed with greyed-out Browse/Introductions nav
6
Note the time and pause here. The admin tester must approve this profile in Scenario 2 before you can browse.
2
Admin reviews and facilitates
Approve profile → handle introduction → share contacts
Manager
1
Log in as admin and go to /admin/operations. The Needs action section should show the profile from Scenario 1 at the top of the queue.
Expected: profile card visible with Pending badge; joined date shows as today
2
Click the profile row to open the side panel. Review the profile details and the Family account section — check contact name, relationship, and email verified status.
Expected: panel opens without page navigation; Family account shows contact name and Email verified in green
3
Add an internal note in the notes section. Type anything and click Add note.
Expected: note appears with your admin name and timestamp; persists on refresh
4
Click Approve.
Expected: instant row update; toast notification Profile approved; pending queue count decreases by 1
5
Have the family user log back in. The being reviewed screen should be gone. They should see the browse page and be able to express interest in a profile.
Expected: full browse page visible; Express interest button works; monthly counter shows (e.g. 1/5 for free plan)
6
Back in admin: go to the Introductions section. Click Facilitate on the interest expressed in step 5.
Expected: both families receive an email; introduction status updates to Facilitated; notification bell updates for the member
3
Super admin oversight
Platform metrics → manager appointment → funnel review
Super admin
1
Log in as super admin and navigate to /admin. You should see the platform overview dashboard — not the operations queue.
Expected: metrics row (active members, approved this week, matches in progress); onboarding funnel; subscription breakdown; manager list
2
Review the onboarding funnel. Numbers should reflect Scenario 1 — at minimum: 1 registered, 1 verified, 1 profile created, 1 approved.
Expected: funnel shows real numbers; drop-off between verified and profile created is visible
3
Go to Managers and click Appoint new manager. Fill in the details and assign a scope (e.g. London, female profiles).
Expected: new manager appears in the list with their scope displayed
4
Navigate to /admin/operations. As super admin you should see the full unscoped queue. Verify the sidebar link takes you there directly.
Expected: Go to operations in SA sidebar works; all profiles and matches visible regardless of scope
Things to specifically watch for — note and report each one
A Any page that shows a loading spinner and never resolves
B Any action button that clicks but nothing visibly changes
C The notification bell — does it show a count after interest is sent and after approval?
D The monthly interest counter — does it show the correct limit for the plan?
E The profile pending screen — does it show before approval, and disappear after?
F Any text that says "coming soon", "alert", or triggers a browser popup
G Mobile: does the navigation work correctly on a phone screen?
H Back to website link — present on registration and verification screens?
